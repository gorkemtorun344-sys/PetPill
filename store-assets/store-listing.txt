APP NAME: PetPill - Pet Medication Tracker

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Track pet medications, health records & vet visits. Never miss a dose again!

FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max):
--------------------------------------
PetPill is the cutest and easiest way to manage your pet's medications and health — all in one app!

Whether your furry friend takes daily vitamins, monthly flea prevention, or post-surgery medication, PetPill helps you stay on top of every dose with smart reminders and a beautiful, easy-to-use interface.

KEY FEATURES:

💊 Medication Tracking
• Add unlimited medications with dosage, frequency, and schedule
• Track daily/weekly/monthly/custom medication schedules
• Mark doses as given, skipped, or missed
• Get reminders so you never forget a dose
• Track medication supply and get refill alerts

🐾 Pet Profiles
• Create detailed profiles for all your pets
• Add photos, breed, weight, and birthday
• Track multiple pets in one app

📊 Health Tracker
• Log your pet's weight over time
• Track symptoms and health changes
• Record vaccination history with reminders
• Keep vet appointment schedules organized

💰 Price Comparer
• Compare medication prices across pharmacies
• Find the best deals on popular pet medications
• Save money on your pet's prescriptions

👨‍👩‍👧 Caregiver Support
• Add family members as caregivers
• Share medication responsibilities
• Everyone stays informed about your pet's schedule

🏥 Vet & Emergency
• Store vet appointment details
• Quick access to emergency poison control numbers
• Export health reports as PDF for vet visits

🔒 Privacy First
• All data stored locally on your device
• No accounts required
• No data collection or tracking
• Your pet's health info stays private

PERFECT FOR:
✅ Pet parents managing daily medications
✅ Senior pet owners with complex medication schedules
✅ Multi-pet households
✅ Post-surgery medication tracking
✅ Chronic condition management (diabetes, allergies, etc.)

Download PetPill today and give your furry family members the care they deserve! 💕🐾
