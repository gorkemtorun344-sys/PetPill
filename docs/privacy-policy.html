PetPill Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

In short: PetPill stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Your pet's health data stays on your phone.

1. Information We Collect

PetPill is designed with privacy first. The app collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Pet Information: Names, species, breed, weight, date of birth, and photos you add
  • Medication Data: Medication names, dosages, schedules, and administration history
  • Health Records: Weight logs, symptom tracking, vaccination records, and vet appointment details
  • Caregiver Information: Names, phone numbers, and relationships of caregivers you add

2. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

3. Camera & Photo Library

PetPill may request access to your camera and photo library to allow you to add photos of your pets. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

4. Location Services

PetPill may request location access to help you find nearby veterinary clinics. Your location data is used in real-time only and is never stored or transmitted.

5. Notifications

PetPill uses local notifications to remind you about medication schedules, refills, and vet appointments. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No push notification tokens are sent to external servers.

6. Third-Party Services

PetPill does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy

PetPill does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is intended for pet owners of all ages, and no data leaves the device.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric lock.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app
  • Delete: Remove any pet, medication, or health record at any time
  • Export: Use the Export Health Report feature to generate a PDF of your data
  • Full Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
